DMCA Copyright Policy

UltraStream operates strictly as a conduit that processes public URLs provided by the user. We do not have servers storing copyrighted audio or video files. All media is downloaded directly onto the user's local machine from the respective third-party CDN.

Filing a DMCA Notice

Because we do not host any media files, we cannot typically remove media from the internet. The infringing media remains hosted on properties like YouTube, Vimeo, or TikTok.

If you believe a user is abusing our specific tool to fetch your copyrighted content unlawfully, you can submit a takedown request referencing the domains in question, and we will implement blocklist rules to prevent our backend from processing URLs containing your proprietary media patterns.

Contact

To file a notice, please use the Contact form or email the administrative team with your official DMCA formatted claim, establishing your rights to the content.
